SWEET AND SOUR CHICKEN RECIPES

Description

Sweet and Sour Drumettes is a delightful dish that features chicken drumettes as the star ingredient

The dish is typically served hot and can be enjoyed as a main course or as an appetizer. Its a popular choice for parties and gatherings due to its delicious taste and appealing presentation. The combination of the crispy chicken drumettes with the sweet and sour sauce makes for a dish that is sure to impress.

In terms of nutrition, each serving contains 487 calories, 19g of fat, 57g of carbs, and 23g of protein. This makes it a hearty and satisfying dish that can be part of a balanced meal. Overall, Sweet and Sour Drumettes is a dish that offers a unique and enjoyable eating experience. Its combination of textures and flavors makes it a favorite among many.

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken drumettes
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour for coating
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup vinegar

Steps

  1. Dip chicken in egg beat, then flour. Fry in a large skillet until crispy, about 15 minutes. Drain and place in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. To Make Sauce: In a small bowl combine the soy sauce, water, sugar and vinegar. Mix together and pour over chicken.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cooked through and juices run clear.
